ABOUT US



Founded in 2001, MediCare started as a small family-owned healthcare store in Kemanggisan, Jakarta. As more and more people get to know about MediCare, it is natural to us to expand our stores to other cities and provinces. Now, we have more than 100 stores spread out in almost every major cities in Indonesia including but not limited to Aceh, Medan, Palembang, Jakarta, Surabaya, Pontianak, Balikpapan, and Makassar.We also offers a wider variety for products for healthcare, cosmetics, medicines, and many more. We have a commitment to stay true to our humble origin and serving our customers with best customer service and high quality products.
